[heading]Description[/heading]
Unlocks the debug menu the developer already built into the game but left disabled. This mod doesn't add anything new, it just flips it back on.

[line]

[heading]Installation Instructions[/heading]
[list=1]
[*]Requires Godot Mod Loader to be installed first.
[*]Place the .zip file inside [b]AboutFishing/mods/[/b] (create the folder next to the exe if it doesn't exist).
[/list]

[line]

[heading]Main Features[/heading]
[list]
[*]Load scenes
[*]Spawn items and fish
[*]Camera controls
[/list]

[line]

[heading]Usage[/heading]
Press F1:
[list]
[*]While in-game: opens camera and cinematic controls.
[*]While paused: opens teleport, item & fish spawning, and story/save controls.
[/list]

When spawning an item, close the debug menu and open your inventory to place the obtained item.

[line]

[heading]Known Issues[/heading]
[list]
[*]Loading a new scene from some locations sometimes triggers an unrecoverable error. Workaround: always load scenes from outside a "Building" scene.
[*]Talking to "Nathan" in the test scene causes an unrecoverable null reference error. His dialogue was reused from Joshua, and the camera referenced by that dialogue has since been renamed.
[/list]
